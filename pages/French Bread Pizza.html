French bread Pizza

French Bread Pizza – the easiest way to make pizza at home, and it’s ready in no time! Made with soft yet hearty French bread, robust, garlicky and herby pizza sauce, two types of rich cheese and all your favorite toppings!

Ingredients

  • 1 (14 oz) loaf French bread (about 17-inches long, 4-inches wide*)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 cup (scant) pizza sauce, preferably homemade, or store-bought
  • 1 3/4 cups (7 oz) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup finely shredded parmesan cheese
  • Desired toppings

Steps

  1. Prep and slice: Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Slice the loaf of bread in half lengthwise. Lay the bread on a baking sheet.
  2. Add sauce, cheese and toppings: Spread evenly with the pizza sauce, top with cheese and favorite toppings.
  3. Bake and serve: Bake for 12-15 minutes or until heated through and cheese is melted. Slice and serve.
